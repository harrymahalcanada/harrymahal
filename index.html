Neoliberalism on Urban Communities

Objective

The objective of this project is to explore and critically analyze the impact of neoliberal policies on urban communities, with a particular focus on crime rates and social inequalities. By examining the systemic and structural effects of neoliberalism, this project aims to raise awareness about the negative consequences of these policies and propose potential solutions to mitigate their impact.
