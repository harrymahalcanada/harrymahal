About Project

The project is to investigate neoliberalism and its considerable role in shaping the urban inhabitantsâ€™ lives. Neoliberalism can be summed up with the policies supporting free market, small government, liberalization, and privatization. Such policies have brought remarkable transformations to the urban societies for instance this has obliterated the difference of public services provision, increased cases of rivalry, statuses standards of living for example has deepened the classes differentiation between the prosperous and the needy. In the case of this topic, where Crim 302 focuses on the economic policies, social injustice, and urban crime, this project is relevant. while the neoliberal approach maybe efficient economically, socially it leads to stripping of certain groups of people, hence increasing social inequality. Thus, in understanding how neoliberal policies reduce the affordability and accessibility of homes and crucial services, as well as limit available job opportunities, the project is interested in discovering the hidden meanings of these policies in relation to crime levels and social equity. In my case that I will be conducting an analysis, the focus will be placed on elaborating these dynamics within the framework of criminology with a view of enhancing formulation of policies that are fair and efficient. Also, it tries to raise awareness of how neoliberalism, which provides individuals with responsibilities, contributes to repeating poverty and crime patterns in an urban setting.
